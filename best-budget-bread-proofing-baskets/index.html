Proofing baskets, or bannetons, are the secret to bakery-style loaves with that beautiful spiral pattern and a crisp, even crust. They support the dough as it rises, wicking away excess moisture so the loaf holds its shape when turned out. For home bakers on a budget, the challenge is finding a basket that won’t fall apart after a few uses but still delivers consistent results. When choosing a budget-friendly proofing basket, look for natural materials (rattan or cane are traditional), a removable liner for sticky doughs, and a size that matches your typical loaf. A coated or glued basket can degrade quickly, so a simple unvarnished rattan is often the smartest choice. We focused on models that earn high marks from owners and expert reviewers for durability, ease of cleaning, and value for the price.
